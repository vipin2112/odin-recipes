Healthy Dark Chocolate

Description

A very healthy recipe for a vegan diet. All 3 ingredients are superfoods so not only is this chocolate yummy, but actually good for you! Serving ideas: dip fruits in it, chill and roll into truffles, press into mold and chill, or make hot chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup maple syrup

Steps

  1. Mix coconut oil, cocoa powder, and maple syrup together in a bowl until unevenly combined.
